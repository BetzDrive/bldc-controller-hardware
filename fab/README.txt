PCB fabrication notes
====
 * 4 layers
 * green soldermask, white silkscreen
 * rectangular, 60mm x 60mm
 * 1.6mm finished thickness
 * minimum via drill 15mil
 * minimum trace/space 7mil

Assembly notes:
====
 * double-sided SMT, heavy parts on top side
 * two connectors with through-hole mounting tabs
 * please ignore all parts with "Do Not Populate" (DNP) part numbers
